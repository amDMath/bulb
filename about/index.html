D—Math
We work on the field of algorithms and Theoretical Computer Science, advancing knowledge through problem solving and fundamental research.
We believe mathematics is about truth and beauty.

The mathematics of modern computer science is built almost entirely on discrete math, in particular combinatorics and graph theory. We believe in building firm mathematical foundations on everything we do, exploring the frontiers of the field. Our mission is to learn and research on fundamental topics and play our roles in expanding the field.

We are a small group of faculty and students at Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham - Amritapuri Campus. Under the leadership of Dr. Bhadrachalam Chitturi, our group members strive towards exploring the domain. We believe deep knowledge in mathematics is essential for a good computer scientist.

We additionally take part in algorithm based competitions like the ACM ICPC to further advance our mathematical and algorithmic knowledge.
